What is a capsule hostel?

What is the difference between a capsule hostel and a hotel?

A capsule hostel offers a more affordable and social experience compared to a traditional hotel. While hotels provide private rooms, capsule hostels feature compact, private sleeping pods with shared amenities like kitchens, lounges, and bathrooms. This setup is perfect for budget-conscious travelers who want to connect with others and enjoy a comfortable, safe, and functional space.
